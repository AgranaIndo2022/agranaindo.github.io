About Us

Anak Nagari Agribusiness is a supplier of spice and fruit products in Indonesia. We are a company engaged in plantations, a variety of spices and fruits produced from plantations with high quality. We manage more than 30,000 hectares of plantations spread across the Indonesian island of Sumatra.

  • At first our company was engaged in plantations which managed various types of spices and fruits which were supplied to meet domestic needs, over time with the increase in plantation area and abundant plantation products we tried to supply products abroad. And after that our company continued to develop products from plantations to meet domestic and foreign needs.

Anak Nagari Agribusiness will serve customers from all over the world and it has become our top priority to meet the satisfaction and expectations of our customers.
